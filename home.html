Gary Kirsten

Gary Kirsten

is a former South African cricketer, and the World Cup winning coach of the Indian cricket team. He played 101 Test matches and 185 One Day Internationals for South Africa between 1993 and 2004, mainly as an opening batsman. His half brother Peter, also played provincial cricket for Western Province and then later for the South Africa cricket team which included the highlight of the Cricket World Cup in 1992. He was appointed as the coach of the South African cricket team.

Mia Hamm

Mia Hamm

is a retired professional soccer player, two-time Olympic gold medalist, and two-time FIFA Women's World Cup champion. Hailed as a soccer icon,[1][2][3][4] she played as a forward for the United States women's national soccer team from 1987–2004. Hamm was the face of the Women's United Soccer Association (WUSA).The first professional women's soccer league in the United States, where she played for the Washington Freedom from 2001–2003.
